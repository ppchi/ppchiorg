title: Monthly Happy Hour
---

Meet up for casual drinks somewhere quieter than a gun range! 💥

Our monthly happy hour is an **open event** which means that allies and other queer-aligned folks who support our mission are more than welcome to join. ❤️

This event is a great place to meet like-minded folks and find someone willing to mentor you on your firearms journey.

Typically for these we'll meet up at a queer-friendly bar around 6:30pm and try to stake out some tables. These are unticketed events and we'll just be in the public bar space.
