Join a class and learn how to own guns safely and legally. 🎉

We offer a variety of classes that will help you begin your firearm ownership journey. Some classes involve actually firing guns on a range while others are done entirely in the classroom to help you understand the basics.

No experience or prior knowledge is expected at any intro class. If you’ve never even seen a gun in person before, these are the classes for you!
