Come learn and practice your skills with us! 🌈

These range trips require **at least an "amateur" or "comfortable"** level of experience with firearms and shooting.

You should already be familiar with handling a firearm, have a valid FOID card, and be able to provide or rent your own firearm. You're also welcome to join up with a friend who has more experience or find a range partner in our [Discord server](https://discord.gg/WJqHqWb3qv).

Typically for these meetups we will grab some food at a casual restaurant nearby the range for about an hour. After that we'll all head over to the range and shoot some guns for an hour before parting ways.
