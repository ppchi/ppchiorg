title: Monthly Range Meetup
---

Come join us at our monthly range trip!

Our monthly range meetup is an **open event** which means that allies and other queer-aligned folks who support our mission are more than welcome to join. ❤️

These range trips require **at least an "amateur" or "comfortable"** level of experience with firearms and shooting. You should already know how to properly handle a firearm on a shooting range, have a valid FOID card, and be able to provide or rent your own firearm.

[Register for the September Range Meetup](https://lu.ma/uoret9sp?utm_source=ppchi-events){: .btn .btn--wide .btn--info .btn--large}

Typically for these meetups we will grab some food at a casual restaurant nearby the range for about an hour. After that we'll all head over to the range and shoot some guns for an hour before parting ways.
