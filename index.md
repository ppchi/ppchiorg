Pink Pistols Chicago is a charitable nonprofit organization dedicated to ensuring that everyone has a safe and respectful place to learn about firearms.

If you're new to firearms, join one of our [intro classes](/events/intro-class) to learn the basics in a supportive environment. If you're not ready for a class, feel free to drop by one of our [social events](/events/social) or the [Discord server](https://discord.gg/WJqHqWb3qv) to get to know us. We do [range days](/events/range-day) at least monthly. Come check us out! 🩷

Most of our events are **open events** which means that allies and other queer-aligned folks who support our mission are more than welcome to join. 🌈
