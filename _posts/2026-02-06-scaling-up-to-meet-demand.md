Hey folks,

Demand for classes and attendance at events has skyrocketed over the last several weeks. We're working hard behind the scenes to scale up operations and try to meet the needs of as many people as possible. Check out what we've got going on!

## Upcoming Events
I'm excited to announce we're repeating our very popular [Range Office Hours](https://luma.com/zvdju4o9?utm_source=ppchi-updates) event this month. You'll spend 30 minutes in the classroom for a crash course on safety and operation and then spend the second half of class firing pistols on the range with an experienced volunteer instructor.

We've also got another four hour [Handgun Intro Class](https://luma.com/aqp3ctji?utm_source=ppchi-updates) available through one of our third-party training partners, Archetype of The Gun. This is a closed-enrollment session of their class only available to Pink Pistols participants.

## Call for Volunteers
We added three new volunteers this year! I'm extremely grateful for the folks who have come out to help us make these events happen. We're actively looking for people to assist with classes, events, and our online presence. If you're interested in helping out, please fill out our [Volunteer Signup](https://ppchi.org/volunteer?utm_source=ppchi-updates) form.

## Finances

We finished up our first fiscal year with a net operating loss of just three dollars! I'm happy to report that we now have enough revenue through donations that we're able to make sliding scale pricing available for all of our events. We're dedicated to making sure people have access to the information they need to own firearms safely regardless of their financial situation.

Want to help us out? Make a tax-deductible [donation on GiveButter](https://ppchi.org/donate?utm_source=ppchi-updates) or buy some of our [awesome merch](https://ppchi.org/merch?utm_source=ppchi-updates)!<br />

As always, don't be afraid to reach out if you have any feedback about the events or if you need help with anything firearms-related or otherwise. Hope to see you all soon! ❤️
