Hey folks! Things have been busy at Pink Pistols Chicago, and I'm excited to keep growing the chapter so we can help as many people as possible on their firearms journey. Here's some updates and upcoming events! 🏳️‍🌈

## ​Merch Store & Donations
​Our merch store is now live over at [store.ppchi.org](https://store.ppchi.org/?utm_source=ppchi-updates) so if you've been wanting to rep our awesome logo around town, now is your chance! All of the proceeds from our branded products directly support our operations and helps fund classes and events. 😎

​I hesitate to ask for donations when our community is facing an impending food access crisis, but if you have some money to spare we are now accepting tax-deductible donations on our website at [ppchi.org/donate](https://ppchi.org/donate?utm_source=ppchi-updates). We've taught over 50 students already in just the past few months, and donations of any size help us continue running those events at low cost or free for those who need it. 🩷
