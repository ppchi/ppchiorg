Please register for our events on Luma if you plan on attending so that you can receive updates and to make sure that we do not overcrowd facilities. Please be aware that whatever name you enter on Luma will be displayed to other people who register for the event. **You do not need to use your legal name.**
{: .notice--warning}

You can [subscribe to our calendar on Luma](https://lu.ma/pinkpistolschicago?utm_source=ppchi-calendar) or [add our events to your Google calendar](https://www.google.com/calendar/render?cid=http%3A%2F%2Fapi.lu.ma%2Fics%2Fget%3Fentity%3Dcalendar%26id%3Dcal-xPYdgTx1eAaLACw) as well.
